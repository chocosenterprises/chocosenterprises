About our company

Established in 1997, Choco's Lechon Manok has been capturing the hearts of Filipinos in the Province of Ilocos Norte with its carefully crafted blend of Filipino and Chinese flavors. Born out of a proud Ilocana's desire to delight her loved ones, word swiftly spread of its unparalleled taste, propelling its popularity in the local business scene. Over the years, Choco's Lechon Manok has become a part of countless Ilocano households, accompanying them through generations' gatherings and celebrations.

Our commitment to quality, tradition, and hearty flavors remains unwavering, ensuring that every dish we serve continues to foster connections and bring joy to our cherished patrons. From our most famous lechon manok, delicately marinated in blend of spices, to our tender liempo and lechon belly with a consistent irresistable crispy exterior, and our traditional pork morton that sparks an insatiable feeling of home, every bite tells a story of passion and care.

Whether you crave for a comforting meal shared with loved ones or an exploration of the diverse flavors of Ilocos Norte has to offer, we extend a warm invitation to join us on a culinary journey that began over two decades ago and continues to warm hearts today.
